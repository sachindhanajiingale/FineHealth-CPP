The FineHealth project began with the primary goal of delivering an efficient and trustworthy patient management solution for hospital residents and former military medical personnel. The system is intended to help healthcare workers prepare for and arrange for essential medical methods and medical resources in order to offer proper patient care for scheduled patients whose information is meticulously maintained in the FineHealth application.
